Our Story

Born of Passion,
Steeped in Heritage

Nestled along the National Highway in Malda, The Royal Delight was founded with a single vision: to bring the lavishness of Mughal courts and the soul of Indian home cooking into one unforgettable space.

"A meal here is not just dinner -- it is theatre, history, and love on a plate."

From baroque chandeliers to hand-curated busts and vinyl records, every corner is a chapter in an ongoing story of artistry, culture, and uncompromising hospitality.
